Bot Collective Human Bridge — Terms

1. Local Capture
When enabled, this extension captures text content from web pages you visit (on whitelisted domains only). Captured data stays on your device by default.

2. Auto PII Detection
The extension automatically detects and blocks capture of pages containing emails, phone numbers, login screens, banking information, or credit card patterns. You can review blocked items.

3. Sharing to Collective
Sharing is ALWAYS manual. You must review and explicitly approve each item before it leaves your device. Nothing is shared automatically.

4. Server-Side Re-Filtering
Even after you approve sharing, the Bot Collective server performs a second PII scan and strips any remaining personal data before storage.

5. Data Usage
Shared content becomes part of the Bot Collective knowledge pool, accessible to other Pro-tier users. You can request deletion at any time.

6. No Login/Private Content
Only publicly accessible web pages should be captured. Content behind login walls is excluded by design.
